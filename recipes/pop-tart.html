Pop-Tarts - An Empowering Breakfast or a Snack If You've Given Up

Perhaps not the healthiest thing America has to offer, but it is still a powerful offering and a relevant mark of pop-ular culture. If you do not like your Pop-Tarts, fear not! No matter how many you've had, you have not had every flavour.

Ingredients

  • Pop-Tarts

Steps

  1. Place the Pop-Tarts in a toaster and cook to desired warmth.

Don't forget to try one frozen, or to use your toaster's bagel mode to make one side crunchy while keeping the other soft!
